SARD Job Planning

The SARD Job Planning module provides doctors with an opportunity to create their job plan in collaboration with their supervisors, and anyone else who needs access to the plan.

The plan allows for any special circumstances, and a handy calculator shows how many PAs have been contracted against the number of PAs worked.
