SARD 360 (Multisource feedback)

SARD:360 is our Multisource Feedback module. The module covers self assessment, colleague - and patient feedback.

The animation above illustrates the different options we offer for handling patient feedback.

Colleague feedback and self-assessment are both collected via online questionnaires.

The SARD:360 module provides doctors with an analysis that compares their scores against national benchmark data. A colour-coded system highlights any areas that may need to be discussed in either an appraisal or revalidation setting.
