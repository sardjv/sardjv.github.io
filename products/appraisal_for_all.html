SARD Appraisal for All is designed to meet the appraisal needs of all staff, within the healthcare sector and beyond. Our system covers all aspects of the process, including appraisal questions, objectives, ratings, and CPD. Its powerful core flexibility means it can reflect your unique appraisal and compliance requirements.

Features

  • Fast and intuitive. Built on the latest user interface technology
  • Highly configurable to reflect existing appraisal forms and processes
  • Advanced search and real-time reporting functionality
  • Comprehensive administration and user management system
  • Secure 24/7/365 web access from anywhere
  • Individual dashboards to plot progression
  • Fully customisable guidance to reflect organisational requirements
  • CSV user importation
  • Custom policies and reports
  • Complete audit trail

Advantages

  • Access all appraisal information via a single interface
  • Simple and intuitive - see an immediate increase in user engagement
  • Improve compliance and engagement rates via comprehensive reporting features
  • Major administrative savings over existing data capture methods
  • Centralised and uniform appraisal process
  • Powerful custom compliance reporting at your fingertips
  • Links to revalidation requirements
  • Best-in-class user and technical support
  • System access from multiple devices
  • Designed by appraisal and software specialists
