Alice Roche

Hi, my name is Alice. I am the Customer Support Team Leader at SARD, overlooking our support team and I have worked here for four years.

I work on the Live chat support Monday - Friday 9-5, I answer any emails and calls that come through also. I go on client trips and meet administrators within trusts and also manually input the MSF (patient feedback) into the system.

I am also the Charity Champion for SARD, representing our company, and organising fundraising for our Charity of the year which is Crisis for 2018. We are really looking forward to raising money for Crisis and will keep you guys posted about what activities we will be doing to raise money, and let you know about how to sponsor us.

I feel very lucky to be a part of SARD. It's a wonderful company to work for, and I'm constantly acquiring new skills and knowledge each day. I love the ethos and philosophy behind this company, and the way I get along so well with my colleagues. I truly believe in what our company does and feel I add a lot to the team.

I studied Theatre at Brit School of Performing Arts and I like to think my people skills, enthusiasm and creativity does contribute to this team. I'm very excited about working for SARD for longer, and being part of the continuing growth and success of the company in the future.
